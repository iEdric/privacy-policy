Privacy Policy
隐私政策

Last updated / 最后更新：May 8, 2026 / 2026 年 5 月 8 日

English

uncling respects your privacy. The current version is a local-first self-recording and cooldown app. It does not require an account, does not upload your records, and does not include third-party advertising or analytics SDKs.

中文

uncling 尊重你的隐私。当前版本是本地优先的自我记录和冷却工具，不需要注册账号，不上传你的记录，也不包含第三方广告或分析 SDK。

Data Collection / 数据收集

We do not collect personal data from the app in the current version.

  • No account registration.
  • No server upload.
  • No third-party analytics.
  • No advertising or tracking.
  • No personalized advertising.

当前版本不会从 App 中收集个人数据。

  • 无需账号注册。
  • 不会上传到服务器。
  • 不包含第三方分析。
  • 不包含广告或追踪。
  • 不进行个性化广告投放。

Local Data / 本地数据

Information you create in uncling is saved locally on your device by default. This may include:

  • Recovery focus objects.
  • Unsent letters.
  • SOS cooldown records.
  • Daily task states.
  • Recovery map and trigger-related records.

Deleting the app deletes this local data from your device.

你在 uncling 中创建的信息默认保存在本设备本地，可能包括：

  • 想松开的对象。
  • 未发送信。
  • SOS 冷却记录。
  • 每日任务状态。
  • 恢复地图和触发相关记录。

删除 App 会从设备上删除这些本地数据。

Permissions / 系统权限

The current version does not request access to Contacts, Camera, Photos, Location, Microphone, Health, Notifications, or Face ID.

当前版本不会请求通讯录、相机、相册、定位、麦克风、健康、通知或 Face ID 权限。

Future Feature Placeholders / 未来功能占位

Some screens may mention future features such as Pro, AI, export, subscriptions, or Face ID lock. In the current version, these are disabled placeholders only. They do not start purchases, request permissions, or call external services.

部分界面可能提到 Pro、AI、导出、订阅或 Face ID 锁等未来功能。当前版本中这些内容仅为禁用状态的占位说明，不会发起购买、权限请求或外部服务调用。

Medical Disclaimer / 医疗免责声明

uncling is a self-support and journaling tool. It does not provide medical advice, psychological diagnosis, treatment, crisis intervention, or emergency services. If you are in immediate danger or need professional support, please contact local emergency services, a crisis hotline, or a qualified professional.

uncling 是自我陪伴和记录工具，不提供医疗建议、心理诊断、治疗、危机干预或紧急服务。如果你正处于紧急危险或需要专业帮助，请联系当地紧急服务、危机热线或专业人士。

Changes / 政策更新

We may update this Privacy Policy when the app changes. The latest version will be posted on this page with an updated date.

当 App 功能发生变化时，我们可能会更新本隐私政策。最新版本会发布在本页面，并更新日期。

Contact / 联系方式

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the support email listed on the App Store product page.

如果你对本隐私政策有任何问题，请通过 App Store 产品页列出的支持邮箱联系我们。
